Three Independent Tasks

Each task addresses a distinct clinical problem. Teams may participate in one, two, or all three — with independent leaderboards and awards per task.

1
📊
Cardiac Parameter & Biomarker Estimation
Develop AI models to estimate LV ejection fraction, and cardiotoxicity biomarkers directly from echo videos.
Primary metric:  MAE
397 patients · 1,600 videos
Baseline: EchoNet-Dynamic
Prize pool €1,200
2
🫀
LV Dysfunction Assessment
Classify left ventricular dysfunction from longitudinal echocardiography using clinically accepted GLS threshold.
Primary metric:  AUC-ROC
397 patients · all timepoints
Baseline: GLS ≥ −16% guideline rule
Prize pool €900
3
🔮
Early Prediction of Cardiotoxicity
Predict future therapy-induced cardiotoxicity from baseline echocardiography alone — before treatment-related changes occur, enabling proactive and personalized patient management.
Primary metric:  AUC-ROC
254 patients · baseline only
Baseline: HFA-ICOS clinical risk model
Prize pool €900
