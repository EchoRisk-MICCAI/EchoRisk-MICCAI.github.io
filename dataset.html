// Dataset

The EchoRisk-MICCAI Challenge Dataset

A specially curated benchmark dataset derived from the EU-funded CARDIOCARE project. It represents the first echocardiography imaging database with explicit therapy-induced cardiotoxicity labels, collected prospectively across 6 European cancer & cardiology centers.

  • 397 breast cancer patients · 1,600 DICOM echo videos
  • Complete Exams · 916 fully paired studies
  • Longitudinal · baseline + 4 follow-up timepoints (3-month intervals)
  • Views · Apical 4-chamber and 2-chamber cine loops
  • Labels · LVEF, GLS, cardiotoxicity status
  • GDPR-compliant · fully de-identified DICOM per institutional IRB
