EchoRisk-MICCAI

The CARDIOCARE Dataset

The first echocardiography imaging database with explicit therapy-induced cardiotoxicity labels, collected prospectively across 6 European cancer & cardiology centers.

  • 421 breast cancer patients · 1,528 DICOM echo videos
  • Longitudinal · baseline + 4 follow-up timepoints (3-month intervals)
  • Multi-vendor & multi-site · GE Vivid iq across 5 countries
  • Views · Apical 4-chamber and 2-chamber cine loops
  • Labels · LVEF, GLS, LV volumes, troponin, NT-proBNP, cardiotoxicity status
  • GDPR-compliant · fully de-identified DICOM per institutional IRB
